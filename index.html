The Reality

The average cost of a data breach is in the millions.

Even when it doesn’t, the damage is real:

  • downtime
  • lost customers
  • emergency remediation
  • legal exposure

Most of it comes from problems that were already there—just undiscovered.

Attackers don’t need zero-days.
They need one mistake.
